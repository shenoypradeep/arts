Vithala: The Living Rhythm of Bhakti

By Vedic Space • July 2025

In every beat of the mridanga and every syllable of the abhang, the sacred name of Vithala echoes. This name—beloved in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and across Bhakti movements—is more than just devotion; it's lived rhythm.

What sets the Haridasa tradition apart is its intimacy with the Divine. Saints like Purandara Dasa and Kanaka Dasa used simple language to reach cosmic truths, through their songs, often sung barefoot before Vithala of Pandharpur.

In this blog, we dive into how chanting “Vithoba Rakhumaayi” sustains both memory and presence—past wisdom and present grace.
