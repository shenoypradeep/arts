Experience Their Sacred Art

Abhang – the Un-broken

Sant Vishnudas Abhang – Short 1
Sant Vishnudas sees Devi Renuka not just as goddess, but as the Mother Divine — rushing with care, sheltering like a Kalpavriksha to every soul in need.
Sant Samartha Rāmadāsa abhang – Short 2
This stirring abhang by Samartha Rāmadasa invokes the grace of Shri Rāma, whose name alone uplifts and purifies. Yet it also serves as a gentle warning — that without inner alertness, the divine will slip out of reach.

Dāsa Kīrtanas: Songs of the Servant-Saints

Kanakadāsa Kīrtana – Short 1
“Nee Māyeyolāgo” rises like a riddle to the cosmos — Kanakadasa’s poem becomes a mirror, where a soul questions illusion, seeking the Self amidst the shifting play of Māyā and the Eternal.
vedamurti jnānamurti bādarāyana – Short 2
This bhajan glorifies Vedavyāsa as Veda-murti, Jñāna-murti, and Nārāyaṇa — honoring the divine sage as the embodiment of vedas, wisdom, and divine consciousness.
