Veshagāraru
&
Hari-dāsaru

Carrying forward the timeless traditions of their forebears, these sacred artists known as "veshagāraru" (ones who put on costumes, enacting traditional plays) and "hari-dāsaru" (servants of hari) bring the wisdom of the saints directly into people's lives.

They go from home to home, singing traditional bhajans and abhangs of the dāsās (lineage of poet-saints), delivering spiritual enlightenment in its purest form.
